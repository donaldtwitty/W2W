About

Anointed conferences: Women to Women Ministry's conferences are designed to be powerful and transformative experiences, where women can encounter the Holy Spirit and experience His anointing in their lives.

Staff guided by the Holy Spirit: The staff at Women to Women Ministry are committed to following the leading of the Holy Spirit in all that they do. This means that the conferences are planned and executed under His guidance, and that the speakers and teachers are chosen by Him.

Women delivered and set free, empowered and equipped: As a result of the anointed conferences, women have been delivered from spiritual bondage, set free to live their lives to the fullest, and empowered and equipped to serve God in their unique callings.

How women have been delivered and set free, and empowered and equipped as a result of Women to Women Ministry conferences:

    Women have been healed from emotional wounds and trauma.
    Women have experienced breakthrough in their relationships and careers.
    Women have been called into ministry and leadership positions.
    Women have grown in their faith and knowledge of the Bible.
    Women have been empowered to live out their God-given purpose.

Women to Women Ministry is lead by the Holy Spirit, and their anointed conferences are making a real difference in the lives of women everywhere.
