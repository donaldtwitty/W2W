Founder & Visionary

Felicia J. Anderson

A wise woman obtains knowledge, uses knowledge, speaks knowledge, spreads knowledge


Felicia started the Women to Women Ministry due to her healing journey. She understands the transformational power of God's love since she has personally experienced the sorrow of brokenness. Her ministry is dedicated to assisting other women on their path to healing and growth, bringing comfort and hope to those in need. Felicia's work has a beneficial impact on the lives of many women, assisting them in finding strength and faith in their journey to wholeness.


Felicia's dedication to the growth and spiritual enrichment of women through her ministry demonstrates her passion and purpose. Her strengths and talents assist people in need, and her compassion provides comfort and hope to those in need. Felicia's work has a significant impact on the lives of many women, assisting them in healing and growing in their faith.

Felicia is a gifted speaker who uses the word of God to bring hope and healing to women. Her ministry provides a safe and supportive space for women to share their stories and begin the healing process. Through her teachings and empathetic approach, Felicia has helped many women find strength and faith.


Felicia is a remarkable woman with strength and compassion. She openly confronts the problematic challenges that women face and provides steadfast support throughout their healing path. Her work as a champion for women is truly making a difference in the world.
